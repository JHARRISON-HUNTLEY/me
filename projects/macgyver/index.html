MacGyver

Decode MacBook serial numbers to reveal manufacturing details and production information.

About MacGyver

MacGyver is a specialized tool for IT professionals working with Apple hardware. It decodes MacBook serial numbers to reveal manufacturing details, helping with asset management and support.

Features

  • Factory location identification
  • Manufacturing date parsing
  • Production year and week calculation
  • Proper error handling for invalid serials
